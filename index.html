Use Case

Inventory Page

On the Inventory page, you can:

  • View and search for inventory items.
  • Add new inventory items.
  • Update existing inventory items.
  • Remove inventory items.

Vendors Page

On the Vendors page, you can:

  • View and search for vendors.
  • Add new vendors.
  • Update vendor information.
  • Remove vendors.

Orders Page

On the Orders page, you can:

  • View and search for orders.
  • Create new orders.
  • Update order details.
  • Cancel orders.

Reports Page

On the Reports page, you can:

  • Generate inventory reports.
  • View trends and analytics.
  • Download reports.
  • Share reports with team members.
